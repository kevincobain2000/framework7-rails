Slider

Framework7 comes with powerful swipe Slider component similar to Swiper with flexible configuration and lot of features:

  • Resistant bounds
  • Scroll prevention in opposite direction
  • Built-in pagination control
  • Carousel mode - allows you to set numbers of slides you want to display at the same time per view
  • Nesting - you can nest Slider in Slider
  • Auto initialization - no need for JavaScript
